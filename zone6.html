About Zone6

How It Began

Zone6 started as a visionary project aimed at blending innovation with real-world application. It was founded with a focus on empowering communities, building inclusive technology, and shaping the future through collaboration.

Initially, it was just a shared workspace in an unused warehouse, but quickly grew into a creative powerhouse where developers, artists, and thinkers gathered to bring ideas to life.

Why Zone6 Exists

Zone6 was created to solve modern problems using technology, design, and community. Its mission is to create accessible spaces and tools for everyone ‚Äî not just coders, but educators, creators, and problem-solvers.

Zone6's Impact

Zone6 is now recognized globally as a model for community-driven innovation. With dozens of successful projects and open-source collaborations, it's helping other cities replicate the model.
